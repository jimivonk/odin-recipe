Quinoa Salad

Healthy and jam packed with nutrition

This bowl of superfood will supercharge your immune system and do all kinds of great things for your body.

Get stuck into this on a regular basis and you will set yourself up for a healthy future!!

Ingredients

  • spinach
  • quinoa
  • garlic
  • ginger root
  • soy sauce
  • asparagus
  • cherry tomatoes

Instructions

  1. rinse one cup of quinoa
  2. put 2 cups of water on hob
  3. put quinoa on hob and bring to boil
  4. simmer for 20 minutes
  5. wash vegetables
  6. peel ginger
  7. grate garlic and ginger
  8. leave quinoa for 10 mins
  9. take 120g of quinoa
  10. mix one tablespoon of soy sauce garlic and ginger into quinoa
  11. put in one big massive bowl and munch it
