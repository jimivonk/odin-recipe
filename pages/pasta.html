Ingredients

  • pasta
  • cheese
  • tomato puree
  • basil
  • salt
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. pour 500g of pasta into boiling water
  2. add salt
  3. grate cheese
  4. squeeze puree into cup
  5. add basil and black pepper
  6. add boling water to cup and mix with teaspoon until consistant
  7. when pasta is ready drain water
  8. mix ingrediant into pasta
