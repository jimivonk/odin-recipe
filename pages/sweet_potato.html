Baked Sweet Potato

Sweet delicious carameliness

Get one of these bad boys in the oven and you are going to lose your mind with how delicious it is.

This is jam packed with nutrients and you could not ask for a tastier treat. You're welcome.

Ingredients

  • sweet potato

Instructions

  1. give it a good wash and scrub
  2. preheat the oven
  3. pierce holes all over to allow even heat distribution
  4. leave in the oven for between 40 minutes and an hour
