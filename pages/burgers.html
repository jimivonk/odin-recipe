Vegan Burger

Get stuck into this delicious bad boy

This one is a show stopper. It is a great mix of nutricious veg and processed naughtiness

Russle this up in no time at all

Ingredients

  • burger bun
  • vegan burger
  • beef tomato
  • small avocado
  • garlic
  • onion

Instructions

  1. fry burger on olive oil
  2. wash handful of spinach and tomato
  3. slice thick slice of tomato
  4. blend half onion and clove of garlic into avocado
  5. Put bun in oven for 3-5 mins
  6. assemble and tuck in
