Salted Asparagus

Bring out the salty goodness in this great veg

It may not be the healthiest way to enjoy this superfood, but I challenge you to find a tastier way!

Delight your tastebud with this treat of salty goodness

Ingredients

  • asparagus
  • olive oil
  • sea salt

Instructions

  1. preheat oven
  2. put olive oil it baking tray
  3. roll asparugus in the oil
  4. generous helping of salt over the asparugus
  5. cook until slighlt burnt
